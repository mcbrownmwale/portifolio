Amazon Sales for Africa (2010 - 2017)

Tools: Tableau

Introduction

Amazon is the word's largest retailer offering a wide range of products, services and digital content. It also provides cloud computing services through amazon web services, which is the largest cloud infrastructure platform in the world. Amazon depends on data for making constructive and strategic decisions. This project has used Amazon Sales data for years from 2010 to 2017 to come up with an interactive Tableau Dashbord that captures insights and trends about Amazon sales in African countries.

Questions

The Dashbord has answered the following data questions:

  1. What is the most profitable Sales Channel in Africa?
  2. Which African country created high revenue for Amazon during the period under study?
  3. Which items fetched high unit price among all items ordered in Africa during the period of study?
  4. Which year sold more units than any other year in the period of study?

Summary of Results

The most profitable Sales Channel has been found to be the offline channel which accounts for 62% of all sales made in Africa during this period. Mozambique created more revenue for Amazon, a total of $3,586,605 seconded by Sierra Leone which created a total of $2,868,580. Mozambique ordered more Household items while Sierra Leon ordered more Office Supplies. Office Supplies have also been found to be asking for a higher unit price of $3,256 in africa than any other item ordered in Africa. In 2012, units sold per year were very high compared to all other years under study. In 2012, a sum of 37, 797 were orderd in Africa.
