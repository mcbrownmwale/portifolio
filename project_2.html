Cleaning, Exploring and Initial Analysis for eBAY Kleinanzeign Cars Data

Tools: Python [ Pandas | Matplotlib | Seaborn ]

Introduction

eBay Kleinanzeign is an online marketplace for classified ads that allow buyers and sellers to meet and connect locally. With roughly 30 million users a month, eBay Kleinanzeign is one of the most important web services for online advertisements in Germany. On average, the company has more than 50 million advertisements listed across numerous second-hand goods categories. A big company like this depends on data driven insights and wisdom for its strategic decisions and planning that is why it collects and store data for almost every activity performed on its website. This project has used data for this company to perform data cleaning, exploration and initial data analysis.

Questions

The following are the questions the project has answered:

  1. What are the popular car brands for the period of study?
  2. What is the most expensive car on average during the period of study?
  3. How do car prices differ by odometer readings on average?

Summary of Results

  1. Volkswagen has been found to be the most popular car by brand.
  2. Porsche is on average the most expensive brand.
  3. Cars with odometer readings of around 50,000 Kilometers are expensive on average than cars with odometer readings of at least 50,0000 Kilometers.
