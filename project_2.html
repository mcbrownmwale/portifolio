Cleaning, Exploring and Initial Analysis for eBAY Kleinanzeign Cars Data

Tools: Python [ Pandas | Matplotlib | Seaborn ]

1. Introduction

This project explores a dataset of used cars listed on eBay Kleinanzeigen, a German online marketplace for classified ads. The dataset consists of secondhand car advertisements posted between 2015 and 2016. The goal is to clean and analyze the dataset to extract meaningful insights about car sales trends, brand popularity, and the relationship between mileage and price.

1.1 Questions

  1. What are the popular car brands for the period of study?
  2. What is the most expensive car on average during the period of study?
  3. How do car prices differ by odometer readings on average?

2.1 Data Exploration and Cleaning

Dataset Overview

  • The dataset originally contained 371,539 rows and 20 columns.
  • A random sample of 50,000 records was selected for efficient processing and analysis.

Data Cleaning Steps

  • Renamed Columns: Converted from camelCase to snake_case for better readability and consistency.
  • Handled Missing Values:
    • Categorical columns with missing values were filled with "Unknown."
    • o Affected columns included vehicle_type, gear_box, model, fuel_type, and unrepaired_damage.
