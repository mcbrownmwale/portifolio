Analysing KickStarter Projects

1. Introduction

Kickstarter is a global crowdfunding platform designed to help creators bring their projects to life. It serves as a bridge between individuals or organizations with creative ideas and backers who provide financial support. As data analysts for a startup considering crowdfunding on Kickstarter, our objective is to analyze past Kickstarter projects to determine key factors contributing to their success or failure. This analysis will assist our management team in making data-driven decisions about which projects to launch.

1.1 Research Questions

We aim to answer the following questions:

  1. What are the success rates across different project categories?
  2. Which countries have the highest success rates?
  3. How does the funding goal affect a project's probability of success?
  4. What is the relationship between the number of backers and a project's success?

1.2 Tools

The following tools were are used in this projects:

  1. Database Management: PostgreSQL
  2. Programming Language: Python
  3. Python Libraries: Pandas, psycopg2, SQLAlchemy, Numpy, Matplotlib, Seaborn
  4. Development Environment: Jupyter Notebook

3. Data Analysis

3.1 Success Rates by Project Category

We analyzed the success rates of projects across different main categories. The top 5 categories with the highest success rates are:

Category Success Rate
Dance 62%
Theater 60%
Comics 54%
Music 47%
Art 41%

Insight:Projects in the Dance and Theater categories are more likely to be successfully funded.

3.2 Success Rates by Country

The top 5 countries with the highest project success rates are:

Country Success Rate
United States 37%
Great Britain 36%
Hong Kong 35%
Denmark 32%
Singapore 32%

Insight:Projects launched in the United States have the highest probability of success, followed by Great Britain and Hong Kong.

3.3 Effects of Funding Goal on Success Probability

We categorized funding goals into four groups and analyzed their impact on success rates:

Goal Category Success Rate
Small Goal (<= $2,000) 48%
Medium Goal ($2001 - $5200) 40%
Large Goal ($5201 - $16,000) 34%
Mega Goal (> $16,000) 20%

Insight:Projects with smaller funding goals are more likely to succeed. The higher the funding goal, the lower the success probability.

3.4 Relationship Between Number of Backers and Project Success

We categorized projects based on the number of backers:

Backers Category Success Rate
Large (>= 56) 82%
Medium (12 - 55) 51%
Small (2-11) 7%
Minimal (< 2) 0.5%

Insight:Projects with a higher number of backers have significantly higher chances of success.

4. Key Insights and Recomendations

  • Prioritize High-Success Categories: Dance, Theater, and Comics have the highest success rates, making them ideal choices for new projects.
  • Launch Projects in High-Success Countries: The United States and Great Britain have the best track records for project success.
  • Set Realistic Funding Goals: Smaller funding goals significantly improve the chances of success. Setting an achievable target is crucial.
  • Engage a Large Audience: The number of backers is the strongest indicator of success. Marketing and outreach efforts should focus on maximizing the number of contributors.

5. Next Steps

For deeper analysis we recommend:

  • Predictive Analysis: Building machine learning models to predict the probability of project success based on input parameters.
  • Prescriptive Analysis: Providing actionable recommendations for optimizing funding strategies.

This project serves as a foundational analysis for understanding Kickstarter success factors, guiding strategic decision-making for our startup.
