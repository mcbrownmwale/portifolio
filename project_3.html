Analysing KickStarter Data

Tools: PostgreSQL, Juypter Notebook

Introduction

For this project, we took on the role of a Data Analyst at a startup. The product team was considering launching a campaign on kickstarters to test the viability of some offerings. We were tasked to pull data that would help them understand what might influence the success of the campaign.

Questions

Specifically, the project was to answer the following questions:

  1. What types of projects are most likely to be successful?
  2. Which projects fail?

Summary of Results

  1. Projects that are well funded are most likely to be successful.
  2. Projects that are Nearly funded and Not Nearly funded do fail.
